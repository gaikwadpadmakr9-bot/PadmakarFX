Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free forever? +

Yes, 100% free forever. The basic AI analytics dashboard, trading journal, and broker integration will always be free. We earn money through affiliate commissions when you trade, and premium features we'll add later (optional). You never pay a dime unless you choose to upgrade to premium signals—and even then, it's optional.

How do you make money if it's free? +

Three ways: (1) Affiliate commissions from brokers when you trade through our referral links—$5 to $10 per lot, for life. (2) Optional premium subscriptions ($29/month) for advanced AI signals and backtesting. (3) B2B white-label deals where prop firms license our dashboard. You benefit, we profit. Win-win.

Do I need a broker account to use this? +

No. You can use PadmakarFX with a demo account to practice and learn. But to earn affiliate commissions and trade real money, you'll need a live account with one of our partner brokers. We recommend IC Markets, Pepperstone, or AvaTrade for best commissions and security.

Is my account data safe? +

Your API keys and trading history are encrypted locally on your device. We never store your money. We never see your account. You control everything. We just help you analyze and earn commissions through your trades.

What if I just want to learn? +

Perfect. Use a demo account. Practice with our trading signals and AI analysis. Build confidence. When you're ready to trade live, activate your broker link and start earning passive commissions on every trade. No pressure. No fees.
